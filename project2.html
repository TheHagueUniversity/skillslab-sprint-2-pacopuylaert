PROJECT 2


Design Factory Calligraphy

calligrapher: Paco Puylaert
model: Guadalupe Merlo
photographer: Giulio Cavicchini
editor: Dennis Fang


THE STORY

Since I was a kid I used to go to these design factories because of my fathers job. I find this places fashinating and kind of magic because here objects get created. Through strange materials and machinery, incredible objects gain existence. By combining the enlighted product from WET design, and the shapes of calligraphies I made, a strong visual effect has been reached. The goal was to find harmony between the bathtub, human body and calligraphy in a different location then where you would usually see these elements.
